# Final Rationale

## Why The New Direction Is Materially Better

### Before

- The product felt like a competent component assembly.
- The homepage behaved more like a landing page with a map behind it than a map-led product.
- Meetings and conferences leaned on repeated cards instead of clearer information hierarchy.
- The tone explained too much while still feeling generic.
- The code allowed client components to reach too close to source/query data.

### After

- The homepage is a true front door: one dominant map surface, controlled copy, and immediate actions.
- Meetings now behave like an explorer, not a brochure.
- Conferences are treated selectively, with one featured record, a readable current list, and static detail pages.
- The dark system feels calmer and more authored because it relies on spacing, contrast, and typography instead of decoration.
- The architecture is cleaner because routes now select data server-side and pass smaller client props.

## The Most Important Improvement

The site now behaves like a product with a point of view. It is less interested in looking “finished” and more interested in making the network legible fast, with enough honesty to admit where the data is still imperfect.
